About Me

To practice any art, no matter how well or badly, is a way to make your soul grow.

Kurt Vonnegut

My name is Carolyn Shetter, and I am an artist. I didn't always find myself creatively inclined. When I was growing up I was told by society that if you weren't good at something you probably shouldn't do it. I found myself giving up on art at 11 when I did a self-portrait I deemed a failure.

I grew to love photography with the invention of cell phones. You will find me taking pictures whereever I go to thanks to my iPhone XR. I've only ever used cell phone cameras, and I enjoy how simple and user-friendly they are.

Water color painting came into my life a little bit later. I found myself in need of a new hobby at 24, and I went to Walmart and bought a water color paint set. While I don't use that paint set anymore, it did help me fall in love with water color.

I am grateful for my second chance at being an artist, and I mostly focus my art on whatever invokes a feeling in me. I like bright colors because I prefer to use my art as a symbol of hope.

Thanks for looking at my art! 11 year-old me is pretty stoked.
